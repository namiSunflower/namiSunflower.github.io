Typography

  • Who: Students, pilots, airline passengers, fishermen, and others who are interested to know what weather it is
  • Age: 15 and above.
  • Income: Open to any type of income, from low middle-income households to the upper income brackets.
  • Motivation: Educate and present most up-to-date information about the weather.
