Target Audience

Persona 1: Daniel Santos

Occupation: Student
Demographics and Education: 20 years old. Nursing student. Peer advisor and consistent Dean's lister in his class.
Social Life: Lives in a dorm with 6 other roommates. Goes to his hometown, in Bulacan, every weekend to bond with family. Enjoys hanging out with his five younger siblings, and normally spends his freetime studying or working to help provide for family's needs.
Technology: Uses a second-hand Acer laptop for most of his schoolwork. Owns a Samsung Galaxy Note 7 and uses it to chat with classmates, browse the Internet, and communicate with his family.
Quote: "Education is key to a bright future. It's important to attend classes daily and make the most out of school lessons."

Persona 2: Robert Karlsson

Occupation: Airline Pilot
Demographics and Education: 46 years old. Senior pilot at a major airline company in Idaho. Has an ATPL(Airline Transport License) and a Bachelor's degree in Aeronautical Engineering
Social Life: Very active and social. Enjoys travelling to other places and trying new things out. Hobbies include hiking and jogging in the mornings, as well as photography.
Technology: Minimalist and uses mainly Apple products. Has a Macbook Pro to check his emails and other work-reated information. Uses an Iphone X as his mobile phone when travelling.
Quote: "Safety is my utmost priority when flying. I excel only in the things I find exciting."

Scenarios:

  • Will the site show me whether or not classes are suspended for tomorrow?
  • I want to see what weather it will be for my hiking trip next month.
  • Should I bring rain boots to school today?
  • Is there a way to see the temperature in both Celsius and Fahrenheit?
  • Can I see an icon representing the weather whenever I'm in a rush?
